About This Course

Welcome to the Computer Forensics course. This comprehensive program covers the essential concepts of digital forensic investigation, from understanding what constitutes a crime to collecting and preserving electronic evidence for legal proceedings.

What You'll Learn

  • Computer forensic investigation concepts
  • Crime categories and legal definitions
  • Types of evidence and their admissibility
  • Electronic evidence vs. physical evidence
  • Discovery process and methods
  • Legal procedures and chain of custody

Who This Is For

  • Information Systems students
  • Aspiring forensic investigators
  • Law enforcement professionals
  • IT security specialists
  • Legal professionals in tech

Course Format

  • Weekly structured modules
  • Interactive HTML-based lessons
  • Bilingual content (English & Arabic)
  • Real-world case studies
  • Legal and technical perspectives
