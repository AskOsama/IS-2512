The 3 A's Methodology

The scientific approach to forensic investigation

📥 Acquire Collect Evidence
✓ Authenticate Verify Integrity
🔬 Analyze Examine Evidence
👆

Click on a methodology step to learn about Acquire, Authenticate, and Analyze

📌 Key Points

  • Sequential Process: Each investigation step must be completed properly before moving to the next to maintain evidence integrity.
  • Chain of Custody: Documentation of who handled the evidence, when, and what was done is critical throughout all steps.
  • Legal Compliance: All activities must follow proper legal procedures to ensure evidence admissibility in court.
  • Integrity First: The original evidence must never be altered - always work with verified copies.
  • The 3 A's: Acquire properly, Authenticate thoroughly, and Analyze systematically for court-ready evidence.
